Frequently Asked Question

What is the recommended brewing method?

Here is our standard steeping method:

  1. Boil 200ml of water to 80 degree Celsius

  2. Put the 10g tea leaves into your brewing tool

  3. Pouring the water into the brewing tool and steep for 2 minutes

  4. Enjoy your tea

You could adjust water and tea raito to your preference.

How long can I keep the tea leaves?

Our tea leaves can be kept for 1 year.

How many time I can re-steep the leaves?

Our tea leaves can be re-steeped up to 5 times.

How to store the tea leaves?

Our tea leaves should be stored in a cool and dry place.

Do you provide or sell samples?

You could ask for the samples through email or head to our Melbourne store to discover all of them.
